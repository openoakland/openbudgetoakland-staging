Open Budget: Oakland visualizes the city's current and past budget data into accessible charts for the public. Data is published by the city. See Oakland's previous budgets, as well as the city's five-year forecast.

Mayor's 2017-19 Proposed Budget

The mayor's proposed policy budget for the next two years was released May 1. City Councilmembers have a series of public forums planned in advance of their June 30 deadline to adopt a final budget.
